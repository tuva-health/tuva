# Official Tuva Maintainers

These are the individuals who helped build the Tuva Project and have taken on responsibility for helping maintain the open source. That does not mean they are solely responsible for keeping these areas up to date, but they understand the parts they steward in detail and generally help review pull requests, shape design decisions, and weigh in on architectural decisions about what should be merged.

## Founders

Aaron and Coco are the co-founders of the Tuva Project. They started Tuva in 2022 after spending more than 10 years building the foundational components of the Tuva core platform across multiple payers, providers, and healthcare technology companies. That experience led them to become convinced that the industry needed an open source approach that anyone could adopt. They first met in Salt Lake City in 2015 at a local squash club, then later worked together at Health Catalyst, where they figured out how much they enjoyed working together, and still do every day.
